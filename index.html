Guide to Being a Pro-Gamer

By: Kyra Grays

Welcome to Your Key to Being Successful!!!

This blog is a daily updated website to help you get your pro-gamer career started!

My goal is to help gamers like you get noticed and jumpstart your careers without the hassle of wasting time on social media. With this blog you will be scouted by not only games companies but you'll also have the chance to speak with other gamers on here to learn even more!

Now let's get gaming!!

Familiarize Yourself:

First thing in becoming a pro-gamer......is just the same of any other skill. It requires you to do research. If you have a favorite game you'd like to stream try watching streamers stream that game on Mixer or Twitch. And don't worry if you don't have all the gear that pro-streamers and gamers have. Often times, that takes a while and as we know, gamer gear is very expensive.
Try watching minor streamers, not only is it a good start, but it gives you an idea of how you will be starting off.

Another good way to familiarize yourself is through chatting with other people like you. A good way to do that is to join clubs.

Here are some examples of streaming websites: Twitch, Mixer

Some good community websites to discuss: Reddit, Xbox Clubs

Join Youtube, Twitch, Or Mixer:

The next step in jumpstarting your career is to make a streamer account! This is the most important step because where else would yu stream? A tip for creating your account is to make your username, or the name that everyone sees, your gamertag, or a gamer name most known you as. If you don't, most people will get confused on who you are.
After joining make sure to upload at least once or twice a week, or stream, otherwise you won't get new watchers and subscribers to your channel.

Have Fun and Be Creative!!!:

Lastly, be creative and have fun! People naturally gravitate to people who they think are funny and know how to have a good time. When you watch streams take notes of what you like and dislike....and make sure to keep those things in mind.

Some tips would be:

To plan some of your streams, like what games you plan to stream
Make sure to check the chat of your stream to see what the watchers and saying and maybe address some questions if they have some
Be yourself, nobody likes a copy, and there is only one of you in the world right?
And remember, TRUE success only comes to those who work for it, so make sure to dedicate time!
